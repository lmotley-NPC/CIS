Tips for Asynchronous Success

  • • Check the course site at least 3-4 times per week
  • • Set a consistent study schedule (treat it like an in-person class)
  • • Don't wait until the last minute – start assignments early
  • • Ask questions! I'm here to help
  • • Connect with classmates through discussion boards

Technical Requirements

  • Reliable internet connection
  • Updated web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
  • Microsoft Office or equivalent (Free via college)
  • Webcam for occasional video introductions

Why Study Computer Information Systems?

Strong Job Growth

BLS projects 10-15% growth in business and information systems occupations through 2031.

Competitive Salaries

Most CIS careers start at $50,000-$75,000 with rapid growth potential.

Career Flexibility

Work in healthcare, finance, retail, government, and bridge the gap between business & tech.

Degree Options at Our College:

  • Associate in Applied Science (A.A.S.) in Computer Information Systems
  • Certificate programs in specific areas (Web Development, IT Support, etc.)
  • Transfer pathways to 4-year programs
